    Social Project

    Our recent menstrual hygiene workshop at a rural school! Empowering young girls with essential knowledge and resources is a crucial step towards breaking taboos and promoting health and dignity. Our contribution includes teaching girls to make reusable cloth pads, offering financial benefits and reducing infection risks. These pads last up to two years, eliminating the need for frequent purchases. By empowering girls with these skills, we provide a sustainable and hygienic solution. This initiative not only addresses immediate menstrual hygiene needs but also promotes economic resilience and environmental sustainability.
